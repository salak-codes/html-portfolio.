The Best Movies According to Enoch

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time!

God is Not Dead

Being a believer of the Gospel, I love how the beautiful story line of this movie encourages Believers not to compromise their faith and to stand for what they believe in -Jesus Christ , regardless of the countless persecution they may face.

Jesus Revolution

Its amazing how this movie teaches on the power of evangelism using a real life story about one of the greatest revivals that happened in the United States of America.

Avengers End Game

Creative,Touching and Action. Genius how this movie passes on a beautiful message about fighting to make the World a better place for the people we love.
